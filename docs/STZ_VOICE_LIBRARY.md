## VOICE — Source-grounded content over polished content

**Rule:** When in doubt between a polished version and a less-polished version of someone's words, choose the less polished one. Real voice has texture, specifics, and occasional awkwardness. AI fabrications are smooth and generic.

**Example good:** "Real estate professional, 21 years in business, focused on buyer agency in the Lake Country area. Part of the Sue Patti Group, which is the team my mother Sue built over 41 years."

**Example bad:** "I am Jason Patterson, a full-time residential Realtor with the Sue Patti Group, helping buyers and sellers across southeastern Wisconsin with honest guidance, steady follow-up, and relationships built to last beyond one transaction."

**STZ source:** Phase 6 Part 0 STZ seed incident, 6/4. Caught the hallucinated version because "relationships built to last" is generic marketing language and "Patterson" was the wrong last name.
