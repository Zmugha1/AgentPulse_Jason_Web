## VOICE — Source-grounded content over polished content

**Rule:** When in doubt between a polished version and a less-polished version of someone's words, choose the less polished one. Real voice has texture, specifics, and occasional awkwardness. AI fabrications are smooth and generic.

**Example good:** "Real estate professional, 21 years in business, focused on buyer agency in the Lake Country area. Part of the Sue Patti Group, which is the team my mother Sue built over 41 years."

**Example bad:** "I am Jason Patterson, a full-time residential Realtor with the Sue Patti Group, helping buyers and sellers across southeastern Wisconsin with honest guidance, steady follow-up, and relationships built to last beyond one transaction."

**STZ source:** Phase 6 Part 0 STZ seed incident, 6/4. Caught the hallucinated version because "relationships built to last" is generic marketing language and "Patterson" was the wrong last name.

## VOICE — Reframe negative metrics as opportunities

**Rule:** When showing a metric that could feel like failure, reframe as an opportunity to act.

**Example good:** "Recovery pool: 69 leads to re-engage"

**Example bad:** "Never worked: 69 (7.9%)"

**STZ source:** L1 Voice library, applied to Market Intel narrative

---

## VOICE — Translate analytics jargon for the realtor reader

**Rule:** GA4 dimension values like "(direct)" or "(not set)" must be translated to plain English with tooltip context for real estate professionals.

**Example good:** "Direct visits (typed URL, bookmark, link from email)"

**Example bad:** "(direct) 76.6% of sessions"

**STZ source:** L1 Voice — realtors are not analysts

---

## VOICE — Footer disclaimers explain what's NOT counted

**Rule:** When a metric excludes something, say so directly below the metric, in Courier slate small text.

**Example good:** "Conversion rate counts real form submissions (chatbot + seller valuation), not newsletter signups."

**Example bad:** Implicit exclusion with no explanation

**STZ source:** L4 Contracts — transparency about calculation method
